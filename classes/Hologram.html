Hologram:setClip( index, enabled, origin, normal, entity )

Updates a clip plane

Parameters

  • index ― Whatever number you want the clip to be
  • enabled ― Whether the clip is enabled
  • origin ― The center of the clip plane in world coordinates, or local to entity if it is specified
  • normal ― The the direction of the clip plane in world coordinates, or local to entity if it is specified
  • entity ― (Optional) The entity to make coordinates local to, otherwise the world is used
